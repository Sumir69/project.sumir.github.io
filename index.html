•AUDIBLE SUNO

Audible Suno is a free streaming service that gives you unlimited access to Audible exclusive audio entertainment. The service is completely free: no payment, no ads, no sign-up required. How do I listen? Download Audible Suno app from Playstore and listen anytime, anywhere.

•Deepstash

Deepstash is a curated platform of good ideas. There are so many things vying for your attention every day, so their proposition is simply this: spend a few minutes a day with them to grow your 'stash'. Download it from PlayStore

•Solo-Learn

SoloLearn is an online and mobile learning platform that offers free coding classes in 13 different programming disciplines. The learning content is jointly created by SoloLearn and, increasingly, community contributors. SoloLearn apps are available on PlayStore and web. The courses are free.

•Home-Workout

As we all are locked down in our houses and physical activity is way too low, so here is an app for home workouts without any equipments.Home Workouts provides daily workout routines for all your main muscle groups. In just a few minutes a day, you can build muscles and keep fitness at home without having to go to the gym. No equipment or coach needed, all exercises can be performed with just your body weight. The app has workouts for your abs, chest, legs, arms and butt as well as full body workouts. All the workouts are designed by experts. Download at PlayStore.
